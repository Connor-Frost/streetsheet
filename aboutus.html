About Us

What we do

Off The Fence, is a registered charity (1108777) committed to making a real difference in the lives of many that are ‘most at risk’ in our community.

We are engaged in four main areas of work in Brighton & Hove:


  1. Antifreeze – Life-saving support for the homeless

  2. Gateway – Supporting Women, restoring dignity

  3. Schools & Youth – Supporting schools in Brighton & Hove

  4. Hygiene for All - Mobile hygiene, laundry and shower vans for the homeless
